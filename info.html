We are finalist students from the University of Reading's art department, (that building that no one knows where it is... but it's just over the lake... no it's not agric), hosting our final degree show on 5th June 2020. The degree show is the culmination of our 4 years at Reading and will showcase over 50 artworks from students, from painting and sculpture to contemporary and conceptual works, installation, performance and film. There will be something everyone can enjoy. As a cohort we are engaged in creating a collaborative event to showcase the work we are incredibly proud of.
